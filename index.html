Approach

We believe that investing for the long run in companies with great, stable, business systems leads to exceptional capital appreciation.

We allocate capital through a quantamental allocation strategy based on our quantitative identification models and our strong fundamental analysis.

Our portfolio is currently weighted toward leading equities in new or rapidly changing industries so that we may achieve our internal capital growth initiatives.

We take a Ted Williams approach to investing and we swing with all we've got at "fat pitches" in our strike zone. Our conviction for our process is evident through our highly concentrated positions.

Mission

Our mission is to advance decision-making theory through the improvement of dynamic system modelling. We believe in order to make a proper decision, one must know all there is to know about the system it operates in - including knowing what information is missing, insignificant, and prone to change. Our company uses the markets we operate in as a feedback system for refining our proprietary decision-making process. Research directing operations. Operations funding research. Improving our understanding of systems theory through the lens of investment management keeps us driven.
