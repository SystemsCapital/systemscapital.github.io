S&P 500 Company Data

This table provides detailed short-term performance metrics for every S&P 500 stock, updated daily for investors and market analysts. It offers actionable insights into daily stock market trends, including price changes and earnings data for recently traded days. Our data collection process leverages Python libraries such as Beautiful Soup 4 and Requests to programmatically retrieve a current list of S&P 500 companies directly from Wikipedia. Daily stock price and earnings information is then obtained using Microsoft Excel’s STOCKHISTORY and LET functions, ensuring the most recent market data is captured accurately. The collected data is converted into JSON format via our Excel-to-JSON tool (available here) and presented in the table below for easy access and analysis. Please note, all information is provided on an “as-is” basis and reflects price-based calculations only; dividend data and other adjustments are not included. This table is ideal for traders, investors, and analysts looking for daily S&P 500 stock insights, short-term stock performance data, and Python-driven market analysis tools.
