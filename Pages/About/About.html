About

Systems Capital was built from a long-only equity strategy based on the idea that if a company makes good decisions, then their stock price will increase over the long term. We are partners, we are researchers, and we are analysts for the companies and industries we invest in.

This idea of anaylyzing a business's systems is our business's namesake. The name is derived from the field of study. System Dynamics. And our approach to research is to be able to understand, and to model, decsion landscapes and the systems that influence these decisions.

We believe our performance is a testament to our ability to identify suitable investment partners.

We're truly a warm team that love what we do. However, we value the privacy of our team, and as we move into a computationally heavy future of the internet, they would prefer not to have their personal information and likeness posted online. We respect that. You won't find our team on Linkedin or other social media.
